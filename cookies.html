This Cookie Policy explains how DuoWeave ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and mobile applications (the "Service").

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website or use an app. They help websites remember your preferences, login status, and activity.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by DuoWeave
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by our partners (analytics, advertising)

4. Other Tracking Technologies

4.1 Web Beacons (Pixels)

Small invisible images embedded in emails or web pages that track:

  • Email open rates
  • Page views
  • Ad impressions

4.2 Local Storage

HTML5 local storage allows us to store data locally on your browser:

  • User preferences
  • Draft posts
  • Cached content for faster loading

4.3 Mobile SDKs

In our mobile apps, we use Software Development Kits (SDKs) for:

  • Crash reporting (Sentry)
  • Analytics (Mixpanel, Google Analytics)
  • Push notifications (Expo)
  • Performance monitoring

4.4 Device Fingerprinting

We may collect device characteristics (screen size, browser version, plugins) to:

  • Detect fraud
  • Prevent spam
  • Improve security

5. How to Control Cookies

5.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete cookies after each session
  • Receive notifications before cookies are set

Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Service. Some features may not work properly.

5.2 Opt-Out of Analytics

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Mixpanel: Mixpanel Opt-out

5.3 Opt-Out of Advertising

  • Digital Advertising Alliance: DAA Opt-out Tool
  • Network Advertising Initiative: NAI Opt-out Tool
  • Your Online Choices (EU): EU Opt-out
  • Google Ads: Google Ad Settings
  • Facebook Ads: Facebook Ad Preferences

5.4 Mobile App Settings

To control tracking in our mobile apps:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Allow Apps to Request to Track (disable for DuoWeave)
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

5.5 Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal. We currently do not respond to DNT signals, but we respect your cookie preferences as described above.
